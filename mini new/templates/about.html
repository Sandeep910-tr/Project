About University Rankings Portal

This portal presents the QS World University Rankings data for 2024 in an interactive and easy-to-explore format. You can browse the top universities, search by name or country, and view detailed metric breakdowns for each institution.

Our Mission

To make university ranking data accessible and useful for students, researchers, and institutions.

What We Provide

  • Browse top universities
  • Search and filter by name and country
  • View detailed scores and metrics
  • Add, edit, and remove entries
